Why should I have to learn about investing?

Investing is easy! Lots of people think that investing is time consuming, hard to start doing, and very risky. Investing is very easy to start doing, it is not at all time consuming, and if done properly, investing is not risky. When I opened up my first brokerage account, I went to a brokerage's website and opened my account in less than 15 minutes. Once you buy securities, it doesn't take any time out of your week to maintain. Put your money in some ETFs and just wait and watch your money grow. The overwhelming large majority of ETFs you can buy are almost zero risk, and grow your money by at least 7-10% each year!

A lot of people seem to think that it is hard to "get your money out" of a brokerage account, once you transfer money into the account. This is false. Imagine you open an individual brokerage account, and on Monday you transfer $500 to the account, you will be able to invest that money buy Thursday. Let's say that on Friday you make $200, and you want to take all of that money out and put it back into your checking account. You sell your investment on friday, and by Tuesday of the next week you will be able to transfer the $1000 + the $200 you made back to your checking account, which will show up in your bank account by Thursday of that week. It is a simple process

The average saving's account, at the bank you use, will pay you an interest rate of about 0.35%. That means that, of the money you hold in that account for the year, your bank will calculate 0.35% of that, and pay you that amount. This is why you should put your savings into an investment account, and buy little to no risk investments, and earn a return rate of 7% instead. Why would you earn 0.35%, when you could earn 7% or more instead? This is why you should invest.
